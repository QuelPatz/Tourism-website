About Ubatuba

A Paradise in São Paulo

Ubatuba is a city located on the coast of São Paulo, Brazil.

It is known for its stunning beaches, rich history, and vibrant atmosphere.

With its lush tropical forests and sparkling ocean views, Ubatuba is a popular destination for both tourists and locals alike.
